Welcome!

I am a visual artist creating artwork that embodies my inquisitive nature and my exploration of the human experience. .

I work from my home studio in Waterloo

In 2007, I graduated from the Ontario College of Art & Design with a focus in painting & drawing. Since then, I have continued to practice and show my artwork locally and abroad. I also enjoy cultivating spaces for artists to create, connect, collaborate and leverage opportunities through my mentorship, teaching and volunteer work.

Check out My Art

I explore Southern Ontario and travel abroad at every opportunity. Each experience of people and place offers new and jarring perspectives which continue to fuel my work. I work primarily in acrylics, charcoal and pastels, though I also enjoy mixed media, inks, watercolours and sculpture. I enjoy skewing scale and proportion within my work to give the viewer a sense of dislocated familiarity.
