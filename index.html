Welcome!

I am a visual artist creating artwork that embodies my inquisitive nature and exploration of physical and emotional places.

I work from my home studio in Waterloo

I enjoy cultivating spaces for artists to create, connect, collaborate and leverage opportunities through my mentorship, teaching and volunteer work. Opportunities for artists include: Life Drawing, Drawing & Painting Fundamentals, Anatomy for Artists, Open Studio, Team building collaborative painting events, Individual or group, online or in person.

Check Out My Art

I am fascinated by the contrasting strength and frailty of nature as it intersects with humanity and technology. I explore spaces and moments where I can feel the presence or absence of people and the passage of time. I enjoy skewing scale and proportion within my work to achieve a sense of dislocated familiarity and perspective. I focus on light and movement to emphasize tension and causality. Each experience of people and place offers new and unique perspectives which continue to fuel my work. I work primarily in acrylics, charcoal and pastels, though I also enjoy mixed media, inks, watercolours and sculpture.
