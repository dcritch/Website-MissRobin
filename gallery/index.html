I am fascinated by patterns and processes. I explore spaces and moments where I can feel the presence or absence of people and the passage of time. I try to capture the tension between moments of transition and change in contrast to stability and permanence within my work. I enjoy skewing scale and proportion to achieve a sense of dislocated familiarity and perspective.

Each experience of people and place offers new and unique perspectives which continue to fuel my work.
